## **Part III — Foundational Prompt Patterns**

### **Module 3 — The 12 Essential Midjourney Prompt Patterns**

#### **1. The Simple Subject Pattern**

“*A single object or character, cleanly rendered.*”

**Structure:**
`[Subject], [lighting], [style]`

#### **2. The Descriptive Stack**

“*Add detail by stacking descriptive layers.*”

**Structure:**
`[Subject], [Five adjectives], [Composition], [Lighting], [Vibe]`

#### **3. The Scene Snapshot Pattern**

**Structure:**
`[Subject] [Verb] in [Scene/Place], [Camera angle], [Lighting], [Color]`

#### **4. The Cinematic Pattern**

**Structure:**
`[Scene], shot on [Lens mm], [Camera Type], [Lighting], [Film Look]`

#### **5. The Character Sheet Pattern**

**Structure:**
`Character turnarounds: [Name], [Traits], front/back/side views`

#### **6. The Story Moment Pattern**

**Structure:**
`A moment where [Event] happens, [Emotion], [Environment], [Action]`

#### **7. The Style Fusion Pattern**

**Structure:**
`[Subject] in the style of [Artist1] + [Artist2], [Medium]`

#### **8. The Product Photo Pattern**

**Structure:**
`[Product] on [Surface], [Studio lighting], [Lens], [Brand aesthetic]`

#### **9. The Flat Illustration Pattern**

**Structure:**
`[Subject], flat vector style, bold shapes, pastel palette, clean lines`

#### **10. The Architectural Pattern**

**Structure:**
`[Building], [Era], [Materials], [Interior/Exterior], [Perspective]`

#### **11. The Moodboard Pattern**

**Structure:**
`Moodboard for [Topic], [Textures], [Palettes], [Materials], grid layout`

#### **12. The Analogy/Metaphor Pattern**

**Structure:**
`Visual metaphor for “[Concept]”: [Symbol1] + [Symbol2], [Style]`
